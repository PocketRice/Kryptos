Looking for more opportunities to explore the cryptography field or bolster your resum√©? Certifications are an excellent option!


EC-Council Certified Encryption Specialist (ECES) This certification comes with a program that introduces the field of cryptography and key algorithms such as DES, AES, and Feistel Networks. Those participating also will get hands-on experience in all sorts of ciphers and will learn steganography.
CompTIA Security+ This basic security certification encompasses a concise, in-depth foundation for any cybersecurity role. Security+ is continually updated to match the ever-changing cybersecurity scene, and is used by many companies to validate these skills. It is a great certification to have if you're considering cryptography as a career, or if you want to learn more security skills!
edX Unlocking Information Security This online course by Tel Aviv University focuses on understanding how to combat vulnerabilities and introduces important cryptographic tools. While not a standalone certificate like CompTIA's catalogue, it provides a professional certificate. It is a great way to dive into the field of cryptography and the larger field of cybersecurity as a whole!
edX Essentials of Cybersecurity This expansive course by the University of Washington focuses on preparing individuals for going into the cybersecurity field (which includes cryptography!) It too provides a professional certificate which is likely to be recognized by most companies/employers. This is a good pick if you want to see if you would be interested in entering this field!
